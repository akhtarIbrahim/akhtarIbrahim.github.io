About Us

McKinsey & Company is a global management consulting firm founded in 1926. We partner with leaders across private, public, and social sectors to help them make lasting improvements to their organizations and the world.

  • Serving over 90% of Fortune 500 companies across 65+ countries.
  • Delivering data-driven strategies that create measurable, lasting impact.
  • Building organizational capabilities that outlast our engagements

With over 45,000 consultants worldwide, McKinsey brings deep industry expertise and functional knowledge to every engagement. We work alongside our clients to solve their most complex challenges — from growth strategy and digital transformation to operational excellence and leadership development.
